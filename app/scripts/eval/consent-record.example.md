## Plain-language disclosure shown before recording

I understand that the meeting audio and a verbatim reference transcript will be processed
locally to benchmark muesly speech-to-text quality, speed, and memory use. The material will
not be used to train models, sent to cloud transcription services, published, or committed to
the source repository. Benchmark reports contain aggregate/error counts and machine metrics,
not audio or transcript text.

I understand how long the audio, reference transcript, and consent record will be retained,
who can access them, and how they are protected. I can refuse without disadvantage. I can
withdraw consent through the contact above as easily as I gave it; after withdrawal, the
session audio, reference, manifest entries, and derived per-sample reports will be removed
unless another documented legal basis requires retention.
